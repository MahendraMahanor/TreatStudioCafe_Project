Add your Treat Studio Cafe photos here so they show in the website gallery.

How to get photos from Google:
1. Search "Treat Studio Cafe Pune" on Google.
2. Open the Google Maps / Business listing (or go to maps.google.com and search Treat Studio Cafe FC Road Pune).
3. In the listing, click the "Photos" section to see customer and venue photos.
4. Right-click a photo you want → "Save image as..." (or take a screenshot if save is not available).
5. Save into this folder as: gallery1.jpg, gallery2.jpg, gallery3.jpg, gallery4.jpg

You can use your own photos too (interior, food, ambience). Replace gallery1.jpg through gallery4.jpg with your files. The site will show these instead of the default placeholders.
